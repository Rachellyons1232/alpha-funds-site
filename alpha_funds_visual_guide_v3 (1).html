00 — System Two accents · Two jobs

Two accents. Two jobs. One system.

Most brands pick one accent and stretch it. Alpha Funds uses two — one cool, one warm — because the brand has two distinct registers to express: the precise, technical, inside-the-sector knowledge of the team, and the warm, human, founder-first voice of J.D. Each accent has one clear job. They appear across the same page constantly, but never in the same headline or sentence.

// Accent 01 · Signal Blue

The instrument panel.

Precision, navigation, data. Signal Blue is the technical register — the cockpit readouts, the mission coordinates, the interactive states. It's where the firm's engineering and financial discipline show up visually.

  • Section labels & numbering
  • Links & hover states
  • Data callouts & metrics
  • Technical metadata
  • Field labels & form focus
SYSTEM
// Accent 02 · Ignition

The engine glow.

Warmth, personality, emphasis. Ignition is the editorial register — the word in the headline that matters, the CTA that invites, the accent that keeps the brand from reading cold. It's where J.D.'s voice shows up visually.

  • Single-word headline emphasis
  • Primary CTAs & invitations
  • Editorial pull-quotes
  • Section rules & ornaments
  • Portfolio signal moments
