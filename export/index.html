// Approach

How we invest.

We take lead positions. Raise around them. Stay close as founders scale.

The market still underestimates space, carrying legacy assumptions about capital intensity and long timelines. In reality, the sector is fragmenting into capital-efficient layers where early, embedded investors can capture outsized ownership in companies that become critical infrastructure.

For much of the space economy, that early moment is now.

Capital is the start. What follows is the work: operational support, sector relationships, time on the ground with founders. We work with the rocket scientists, propulsion engineers, and defense leaders inside the sector. Those relationships come with every company we back.
